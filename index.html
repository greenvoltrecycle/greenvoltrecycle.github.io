About Us

GreenVolt Recycle is a grassroots battery waste recovery initiative based in Hyderabad. We collect, sort, and channel spent batteries to certified recyclers, creating green-tech jobs for underprivileged youth.

Our Services

  • Battery collection from EV & UPS workshops
  • Safe sorting by chemistry (Li-ion, Pb-acid, NiMH)
  • Logistics coordination with certified recyclers
  • Training & employment programs for local youth
  • CSR & EPR partnership support
